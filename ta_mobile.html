If you are a Gen X'er or a Millenial, there is a great chance that your travel decisions are made purely online. You get influenced by your friends, the travel blogs and digital media. Keeping this in mind, I wanted to design a one stop destination where users could share and discover travel stories with their connections and also buy and sell used travel related stuff in their locality. This app has a better understandability with additional features such as speed limit on the road, vehicle average, driver points and weather forecast (now implemented by Waze).
